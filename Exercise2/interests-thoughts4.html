Eternal enemy

The curiousest of all, was a marble part of an ancient column, laid in front of a junk shop, and used as a table for the backgammon-maniac shopowner. Now the shop has closed, and the shopkeeper possibly is not alive; but the column still stands there, too stubborn to surrender to the eternal omnipotent enemy, the time!
