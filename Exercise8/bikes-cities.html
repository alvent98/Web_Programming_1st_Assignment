Bicycles & Cities

In these cities, it is a common sight to see someone riding on a bike, both in the avenues ‎of the city, and in the smaller, unimportant roads. Many keep their old bikes, bikes that ‎sometimes date the pre-war era. And even on, they don’t preserve them as a relic of the ‎past, but as a fully operative mean of transportation.‎

‎ As one can assume, with so many bicycles crisscrossing the roads of these cities, the ‎stealing of bicycles is a huge problem.

Both because it deprives the residents’ mean of transport, but also due to the fact that ‎the stealers can easily find someone who is in need of a cheap bicycle.‎

‎ So, in these cities there is a big semi-legal market of cheap bicycles, that had many ‎owners in the past, and it is considered a great stupidity for someone to leave his bicycle ‎unattended, even when properly chained. The odds are not with him, but with the thief ‎who always waits behind the corner.‎
